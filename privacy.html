Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly how this site handles your data.

Last updated: April 2026
Short version: This portfolio does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. It's a personal portfolio — not a data business.
Data We Don't Collect
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.
  • We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information automatically.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.
Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • No third-party analytics platforms (e.g. Google Analytics) are currently active on this site.
Third-Party Services

  • I am not responsible for how these third-party services handle your data when you visit them.
Policy Updates
This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the site after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
Questions?
